Education for All

The “Education for All” initiative ensures that every Filipino child has access to quality education. Through scholarships, free school kits, and teacher support, the program bridges educational gaps nationwide.

Highlights include:

  • Scholarship grants for underprivileged students
  • Distribution of free books and supplies
  • Training programs for teachers
  • Partnerships with LGUs to support local schools
