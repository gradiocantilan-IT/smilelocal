We Stand with Inday Sara!

Why We Support Sara Duterte

Vice President Sara Duterte, fondly known as Inday Sara, has been a dedicated public servant, championing education, women's empowerment, and community development. Her leadership as the former mayor of Davao City and her initiatives like the "Mag Negosyo Ta 'Day" program showcase her commitment to uplifting marginalized sectors and fostering a business-friendly environment for all Filipinos.

With a strong track record in public service and a vision for a united Philippines, Sara continues to inspire millions. Join us in supporting her journey to make a positive impact for our nation!
